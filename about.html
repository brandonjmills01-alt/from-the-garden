About From The Garden

Grown from community.

From The Garden started with a simple idea: fresh flowers should be easy to find, easy to afford, and rooted in the neighborhoods where people actually live.

Our Story

Built inside
the community.

We built a boutique flower bar that meets people where they are — inside the wellness studios, hookah lounges, coffee shops, and farmers markets that already make up the fabric of DMV life.

We source exclusively through Potomac Floral Wholesale — fresh, seasonal, and fairly priced. Every arrangement is designed, built, and delivered with care.

From The Garden is rooted in the DMV. We bloom where our community gathers.
